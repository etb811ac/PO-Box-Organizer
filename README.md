<h1 align="center">📦 PO Box Organizer</h1>

<p align="center">A simple web app for a group of friends sharing one US PO Box — track who ordered what, what's arrived, what's still pending, and who has paid.</p>

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-WIP-yellow">
</p>

## The problem

We're a group of friends sharing a single PO Box in the US. With multiple packages arriving constantly, it's a headache to keep track of who ordered what, who's paid, and what needs attention first.

## The idea

A small, shared tool to streamline the chaos:

- 📥 Log incoming packages and who they belong to
- ⏱️ See what's pending and prioritize urgent deliveries
- 💸 Keep tabs on who has paid
- 🤝 No more lost packages or misunderstandings

## Status

🚧 **Work in progress** — this repo currently holds the concept and is being actively shaped. Check back as it grows.
